Education

Children age 4 or turning 4 are eligible for Pre-K. All residents have the right to attend public school from age 5 until graduation or until they turn 21. Classes are available for English learners.

Health Care

Low-cost emergency and non-emergency health care is available. NYC Well is a free, confidential, in more than 200 languages, any day.

Child Care

Low-income families with children age 6 weeks through 12 years old can get free or low-cost child care.
